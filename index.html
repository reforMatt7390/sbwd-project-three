Who am I?

Hi, I'm Matt! I’m currently a member of the SGA Student Life Committee, where our team is working on the Student Organization Accessibility Project. At my previous college, I held numerous positions including Service Chair, Fellowship Chair, and Vice President at an award-winning chapter of Phi Theta. I’ve served as a leader inside the classroom as a Supplemental Instructor.

My teamwork and leadership experience extends into the professional world too. I took a break from college and spent a few years managing large hotels and restaurants. There, I gained professional experience leading small and large teams, throwing lit parties/event planning, balancing P&L/financial statements, and managing the overall execution of a multimillion dollar department at a first-class hospitality firm. I have a unique combination of leadership and team experience in both academic and professional settings.
