Why should you vote for me?

Although there are many issues I'd like to see addressed, here are few topics that my constituents have brought to my attention:

Leadership

It's no secret that SGA has had its fair share of setbacks. I'd like to start by addressing the shortcomings within our Bylaws and Constitution. My recently proposed Governmental Affairs Committee was overwhelmingly approved and will address these issues quickly and thoroughly.

Additionally, the External Affairs Subcommittee is tasked with representing the Illinois Tech SGA locally, regionally, or even nationally in community service and collaboration with organizations outside of IIT.

Integrity

Transparency. Students have voiced many concerns about the lack of transparency between SGA and student body. I hope to address this issue by inviting students without SGA affiliation to participate in committees, and releasing any/all information (not deemed confidential) to the student body, encouraging more open dialogues. Although the Exec Board meets in private, I also encourage them to post minutes on any non-confidential matters.

SGA Voting Records. Senators are responsible for representing the interests of their college, and the student body must be able to hold senators accountable! I would like to implement a voting record (via iClickers) so that our senators votes can be recorded and made available to the students. At any time, the student body could review a senators voting record to ensure that their interests are being represented by their senators and hold SGA accountable.

Social Responsibility

Student orgs should be equally accessible to ALL students, even those who come from low-income families, are the first in their family to attend college, or come from other underserved communities.

Start a bonus points "drive" (like a food drive... but instead of collecting canned goods we'll collect bonus points and/or non-perishables purchased with bonus points).
